### Steps

Step 1: Decode from decimal. This provides a hexadecimal string.

Step 2: Reverse the hexadecimal string and convert from hex to base64.

Step 3: Serpent (CFB) decrypt with key "Zombies" and hexadecimal IV `30303030303030303030303030303030`.

Step 4: Substitution cipher with alphabet `ZOMBIESACDFGHJKLNPQRTUVWXY`.
