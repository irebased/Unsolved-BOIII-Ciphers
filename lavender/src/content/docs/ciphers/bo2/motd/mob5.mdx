## Solution

This cipher is an ADFGX cipher.

- Transposition key: `ZOMBIE`
- Substitution mapping: `ABCDEFGHIKLMN -> DRINGESTFAKCH`

Step 1: remove one of the "FDGFFG" lines

Step 2: Apply transposition key

Step 3: Map each pair to the first unused letter in English alphabet (Excluding J)

Step 4: Apply substitution mapping

## Plaintext

> Dringend der Riese ist in Frankreich

> *Translation: Urgent the giant is in France*
