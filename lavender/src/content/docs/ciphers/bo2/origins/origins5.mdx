## Solution

This cipher is morse code that, when transcribed, presents a Vigenere cipher. Each
line of the cipher is decoded separately with the key `INFERNO`.

## Plaintext

> Warn Messiness

> Something blue in the Earth

> Not clay

> We grew weak

> Thought it was flu

> Men became beasts

> Blood turned to ash

> Liberate tute de infernis
